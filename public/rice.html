🌾 About Rice

Rice is one of the world's most important staple crops, providing food for billions. It grows best in warm, wet conditions and is a major source of carbohydrates.

☀ Growing Conditions

  • Temperature: 20°C – 35°C
  • Soil: Fertile, clay or loamy soil with good water retention
  • Rainfall: 1000–2000 mm annually
  • Sunlight: Full sunlight

🌱 Planting Guide

  • Use high-quality seeds or seedlings
  • Transplant seedlings at 20–30 days old
  • Spacing: 20 cm between plants
  • Maintain flooded fields during early growth

🐛 Pest & Disease Control

  • Rice blast disease
  • Brown planthopper
  • Regular water management to prevent pests
  • Use disease-resistant varieties

🥔 About Potatoes

Potatoes are a major food crop grown in cool climates. They are rich in carbohydrates and provide energy for many communities.

☀ Growing Conditions

  • Temperature: 15°C – 20°C
  • Soil: Loose, well-drained sandy loam soil
  • Rainfall: Moderate rainfall
  • Sunlight: Full sunlight

🌱 Planting Guide

  • Use certified seed potatoes
  • Plant depth: 10–15 cm
  • Spacing: 30 cm between plants
  • Hill soil around plants as they grow

🐛 Pest & Disease Control

  • Late blight disease
  • Colorado potato beetles
  • Practice crop rotation
  • Use disease-free seeds

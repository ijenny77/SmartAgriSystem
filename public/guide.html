Maize

A staple food crop grown widely for human consumption and animal feed. Learn about growing conditions, planting, pests, and harvest tips.

🌱 Growing Conditions

Temperature 18–27°C
Rainfall 500–800mm
Soil Loamy soil
Sunlight Full sun

🌿 Planting Information

Detail Information
Season March–April
Spacing 25 cm
Germination 7–10 days
Harvest 3–4 months

🐛 Common Pests

Fall Armyworm Damages leaves and causes holes. Controlled using proper pesticides.
Aphids Suck sap from leaves. Managed using natural predators or insecticidal soap.

📌 Quick Facts

  • Grows best in warm climates
  • Ready to harvest in 3–4 months
  • Important staple food crop worldwide

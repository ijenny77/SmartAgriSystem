🌿 About Beans

Beans are a rich source of protein and are widely grown in many African countries. They improve soil fertility because they add nitrogen to the soil.

☀ Growing Conditions

  • Temperature: 18°C – 30°C
  • Soil: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Rainfall: Moderate rainfall
  • Sunlight: Full sunlight

🌱 Planting Guide

  • Plant seeds 2–3 cm deep
  • Spacing: 30–50 cm between rows
  • Water regularly but avoid waterlogging
  • Remove weeds regularly

🐛 Pest & Disease Control

  • Watch for aphids and bean beetles
  • Use natural pesticides when possible
  • Practice crop rotation
